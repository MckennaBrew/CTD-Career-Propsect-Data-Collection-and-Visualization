Armon Naeini

Class of Fall 2019

UX/UI Designer & Developer at FuegoUX

Why did you decide to study CTD?

I started as a computer science major at CU, but was deeply unfulfilled. After finding out that I could combine my artistic and creative passion with my engineering mindset as a student in the CTD program, I instantly fell in love. I was able to harness my technical, radical and creative skills all at the same time. Creative technology is the sh*t.

Looking back, what about the CTD program has helped you most in your current job position?

Through the CTD program, I was able to explore a plethora of different focus paths within the realm of creative technology. My courses in UI/UX design and development prepared myself for an internship, full-time position and many freelance / contract opportunities as a UI/UX designer and developer/creative technologist.

Can prospective students reach out to you regarding the CTD program?

Yes, via email or LinkedIn.
